NOTES
======
- Data marked with * indicates rates are unreliable (based on small counts)
- Values of "<10" or "--" indicate suppressed data (fewer than 10 deaths)
- Rates are unadjusted suicide rates per 100,000 person-years unless noted
- VetPop population numbers should be reported to nearest 1,000 per VA guidance
- The Excel files contain multi-sheet workbooks with additional detail beyond what was extracted
